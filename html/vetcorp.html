VETCORP

The Putnam County VetCorps Program was established in April 2014 in partnership with the Community Anti-Drug Coalitions of America (CADCA), in order to:

  • assess needs of veterans and military families
  • form a sustainable coalition of individuals and groups interested in helping veterans and military families, and
  • develop an action plan to address identified needs
