Prescription Drug Safety

The NCADD Putnam and the Putnam County Communities That Care Coalition, in partnership with the American Medical Association, will be implementing Prevention for Putnam Prescription Drug Abuse Awareness project. The project will work to promote and establish healthy behaviors amongst our youth. The Healthy Living Grant Program that funds this project supports health education programs to develop school and community-based solutions to behavioral health challenges. This project includes community assessment surveying, radio advertising, movie theater public service announcements, school based curriculum's, and social media outreach.
